Crimson Canvas

About the Piece

Year: 2024

Medium: Oil on Linen

Dimensions: 48 x 60 inches

This piece marked a turning point in my observation of the urban environment. It explores the tension between brutalist architecture and the fleeting, natural beauty of a sunset reflected on concrete. The title refers to the intense use of cadmium red to evoke a sense of controlled chaos.
