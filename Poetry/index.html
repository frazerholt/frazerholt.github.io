I hope you enjoy my poems. My voice and verse is composed using a structured system on Microsoft Excel. This methodology governs the technical elements, including formulas for counting stressed/unstressed syllables, meter, and defining the rhyme scheme and stanza structure. Rhymes and near-rhymes are visually tracked and coordinated using spreadsheet color-coding.
