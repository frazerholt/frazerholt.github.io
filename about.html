About Frazer

My Life and Work

Born in 1985 in Edinburgh, my artistic journey began not with a paintbrush, but with a forgotten typewriter. For years, writing remained the core of my expression, detailing the shifting landscapes of urban life and the quiet dramas of human connection.

It wasn't until a move to the coast in 2010 that visual art took hold. The sudden shift from city grit to ocean light forced a different kind of observation. I began translating the rugged texture of my poems into oil and acrylic, finding that the two forms—text and canvas—spoke to each other.

Today, my work focuses on exploring the contrast between rigid structure and organic chaos, whether through the rhythm of a short story or the harsh lines of a landscape painting. I currently live and work in Melbourne, Australia.
